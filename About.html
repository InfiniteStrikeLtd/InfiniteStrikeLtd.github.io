History

History time! Infinite Strike was created in May of 2010 as a project of an 11 year old boy, he was interested in Anime and Manga. So one day he thought it would be cool to make stories and sell them to his friends. He created Draven's Anime And Manga. He sold some copies but he feared that his school would not like this and since his name was on the books he feared that they would be tracked back to him so he proceed to rename his comic company. He went thorough many names like XComicX and other things of that matter, but he wanted something strong that would as powerful, like a sword that was swung infinitely. An Infinite Strike, he named his company Infinite Strike Manga or ISM. Later he found the internet and began making YouTube content and was called ISMedia, he didn't do well. later he got into programming and wasn't very good and started making games and other stuff like that. Finally he created Infinite Strike. I created Infinite Strike. This small start-up went from an 11 year-old's dream to a reality.
Thank you - Draven Lewis (Ceo)
